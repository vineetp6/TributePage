Srinivasa Ramanujan

Srinivasa Ramanujan's time line:

  • 1887 - Born in Madras, India
  • 1904 - He graduated from Town Higher Secondary School
  • 1909 - On 14 July, Ramanujan married Janaki (Janakiammal)
  • 1914 - Ramanujan departed from Madras aboard the S.S. Nevasa on 17 March
  • 1918 - At age 31 Ramanujan was one of the youngest Fellows in the Royal Society's history
  • 1918 - On 13 October he was the first Indian to be elected a Fellow of Trinity College, Cambridge
  • 1920 - He died at the age of 32
